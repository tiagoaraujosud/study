The Best Movies According to Tiago

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Braveheart

Braveheart is a 1995 American epic historical drama film directed by, produced by, and starring Mel Gibson.

Green Mile

The Green Mile is a 1999 American fantasy drama film written and directed by Frank Darabont and based on Stephen King's 1996 novel of the same name.

The Shawshank Redemption

The Shawshank Redemption is a 1994 American prison drama film written and directed by Frank Darabont, based on the 1982 Stephen King novella Rita Hayworth and Shawshank Redemption.
